About Me

Vivien Ericca I. Hermoso is a fourth-year Computer Science student at Mapúa Malayan Colleges Laguna. She has focused on front-end and mobile development, with a keen interest in software Quality Assurance (QA).

Vivien gained significant experience in mobile development through her work on key projects, including iOS development for Mapúa MCL's digital passport scanner for iPad and Android development for e-ganapp.

She has consistently demonstrated academic excellence as a Dean's Lister. Vivien currently serves as Publication Committee Chair of Mapúa MCL-ACM, 4th Year Representative of JPCS Mapúa MCL, and Internal Relations Associate of Mapúa MCL-SSC. Previously, she was Vice President for Audit of Mapúa MCL-SSC and SSC Representative of Mapúa MCL-CCIS SC.

Through her academic and extracurricular efforts, Vivien has shown a strong commitment to her field and community.

The first task assigned to us interns was to individually learn and develop a website using MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js (MERN). Sir Lex provided training materials for us to follow. By the fourth week, our groups were assigned to create a web application for senior citizens, featuring both client-side and admin-side components.

During the development phase, I primarily worked on the admin side of the application. In the last week, I shifted my focus to the customer side, where I worked on the profile view page and front-end enhancements.
